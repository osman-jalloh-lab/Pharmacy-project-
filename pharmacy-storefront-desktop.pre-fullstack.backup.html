Common questions

Before you inquire.

Does a product card mean the medicine is available?

No. Stock changes frequently. Add the product to an inquiry and ask the pharmacy to confirm current availability.

Can I choose medicine based on this catalogue?

No. This page supports product discovery and identification, not diagnosis or treatment selection. Ask a qualified healthcare professional for advice.

Why might the package I receive look different?

Packaging, manufacturer, brand, strength, and pack size can vary by supplier and region. Confirm the exact product details with the pharmacy.

Does sending an inquiry place an order?

No. The page has no submission service. Copying an inquiry only prepares text for you to share through a verified contact channel.
